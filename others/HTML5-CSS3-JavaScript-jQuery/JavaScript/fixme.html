The Jungle!

The air is electric with the buzzing of bartering and conversations. Negotiations in various languages with wild gestures and all manners of feigned delight and calculated disinterest. While brushing past the array of sounds and salty scents you realize for the first time that your pocket feels lighter. You've been pickpocketed. You wheel around, in desperate hope that the thief would be easy to spot, running away like in the movies. In reality, they could have run off in any direction.

To the north you can smell the sickly sweet of the candy vendors.
To the east people seem to be coming and going with bolts of cloth.
To the west you can hear the racous chattering of hammers on anvils.
To the south is the gate from which you've entered the market.

A child has come up you to, trying to sell you some manner of chewing gum.

Turn on the lights!
